About Me

I am a post-doctoral research associate at the Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics in Garching, Munich working on multiple star systems with a particular focus on tidal interactions. In 2019 I finished my PhD in computational theoretical astrohpysics at the Institute of Astronomy, University of Cambridge.

In addition to a lifelong passion for astrophysics I am a keen follower of bass-driven underground music and enjoy staff spinning and other flow arts.

I am currently exploring the capacity of music festivals as a place to conduct science outreach
