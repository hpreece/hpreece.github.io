About Me

I recently finished my PhD in computational theoretical astrohpysics at the Institute of Astronomy, University of Cambridge. In March I will be starting work as a post-doctoral research associate at the Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics in Garching, Munich. In addition to a lifelong passion for astronomy I am a keen follower of underground music and enjoy staff spinning and other flow arts. I am currently exploring the capacity of music festivals as a place to conduct science outreach
